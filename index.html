The Navarra International Film Festival —NIFF— calls a Short Film Script Contest

In its second edition, the Navarra International Film Festival will award the Melitón for Best Short Film Script to the winning work in the I Short Film Script Contest convened by the NIFF, whose deadline for admission of works opens this Friday, February 7 and will remain open until May 30, 2020. Written scripts for documentary, fiction or animation, original and in Spanish, may participate in this contest. They will consist of a maximum of 10 pages, must conform to the professional standardized format and will be accompanied by a plot synopsis of no more than 10 lines. The scripts must be unpublished, may not have been previously awarded and will have to deal with one or more of the themes that articulate the programming of the NIFF.

Registration for the film workshop to be given by Oliver Laxe is extended until March 15

The registration period in the filmmaking workshop "Filming in Navarra with Oliver Laxe" is extended until March 15. This workshop will be held between June 4 and 13, 2020 at the Melitón Studios, recently opened at the Lekaroz Business Campus, in the north of Navarra. This is the second film workshop organized by the Melitón Studios and the International Film Festival of Navarra (NIFF), after the workshop “Filming in Navarra with Asghar Farhadi”, taught by the Oscar-winning Iranian director in May and June 2019. As in the Farhadi workshop the objective of this workshop is to train and work in the comprehensive production of short films under the advice and support of Oliver Laxe, one of the most outstanding filmmakers in the international film community after signing one of the films of the year: "O que arde".

Cineteca Madrid hosts “La Vida” by Asghar Farhadi, shot in his film workshop at Estudios Melitón

The Navarra International Film Festival and Estudios Melitón collaborate with the series “El Séptimo en Cineteca” on the programming of two short films: “La Vida” (Asghar Farhadi, 2019) and “La tierra calling a Ana” (Fernando Bonelli, 2019). The screening will take place on Tuesday, February 25, at 6:00 pm, in the Sala Azcona of Cineteca Madrid in a session titled "In short ... it is more intimate" where the short films "Carne"(Camila Kater, 2019) , "After Also" (Carla Simón, 2019) and "Golden Legend" (Ion De Sosa, Chema García Ibarra, 2019) will also be screened. In "Life", the two-time Oscar-winning Iranian filmmaker Asghar Farhadi uses an old fisherman to propose a metaphor for life. This short film was shot by Farhadi within the framework of the film workshop "Filming in Navarra with Asghar Farhadi" developed in June 2019 in the surroundings of the first film studios built in the Foral Community.
